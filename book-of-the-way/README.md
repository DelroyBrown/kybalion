# The Book of the Way

A living collection of observations, verses and parables about being human.

## Book I — Time

1. Every person enters the world with a measure of time, yet no one knows the measure they have been given.

2. Tomorrow is a hope, never a promise.

3. The wise do not count their days; they make their days worth counting.

4. Many wait for the right day, not knowing they are living it.

5. Many spend their lives preparing for tomorrow, forgetting that life is happening today.
